    WCAG checklist

    • Keyboard navigation — PASS: native controls, logical DOM order, and focusable summaries.
    • Visible focus — PASS: high-contrast outlines on links, inputs, and buttons.
    • Error summary — PASS: failed submits focus an alert summary with field links.
    • aria-describedby — PASS: help and error text are connected to inputs.
    • Contrast AA — PASS: dark text, strong error red, and primary indigo meet AA on light surfaces.
    • Screen reader support — PASS: semantic landmarks, labels, alerts, and live status regions are included.
